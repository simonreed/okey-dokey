## 2025 Annual General Meeting

**Date**: Thursday 28th November 2025, 7:30pm
**Venue**: Riverside Stadium Clubhouse
**Attendees**: 47 members present

### 1. Welcome
Chair Margaret Chen welcomed members and confirmed the meeting was quorate.

### 2. Apologies
Apologies received from: P. Williams, S. Turner, J. Cooper

### 3. Minutes of 2024 AGM
The minutes of the 2024 AGM were approved as an accurate record.
*Proposed: R. Williams. Seconded: D. Patterson. Carried unanimously.*

### 4. Chair's Report
Margaret Chen presented the Chair's report, highlighting:
- Successful centenary celebrations throughout 2023/24
- Women's First Team winning County League title
- Academy continues to produce first-team players
- Increased membership (now 312 members, up from 278)
- Clubhouse refurbishment completed

### 5. Treasurer's Report
David Patterson presented the financial report:

| | 2024/25 | 2023/24 |
|---|---------|---------|
| Income | £142,500 | £128,300 |
| Expenditure | £138,200 | £125,800 |
| Surplus | £4,300 | £2,500 |
| Reserves | £45,600 | £41,300 |

Key points:
- Bar income up 15% following refurbishment
- Matchday income stable
- Energy costs remain challenging
- Reserves now meet 3-month operating target

*Report adopted. Proposed: M. Chen. Seconded: S. O'Brien. Carried unanimously.*

### 6. Election of Directors
Three positions available. Nominations received:
- Robert Williams (standing for re-election)
- Sophie Turner (standing for re-election)
- James Mitchell (new nomination)
- Sarah Collins (new nomination)

Results of ballot:
1. Robert Williams — 42 votes (elected)
2. Sophie Turner — 38 votes (elected)
3. Sarah Collins — 31 votes (elected)
4. James Mitchell — 28 votes

### 7. Appointment of Auditors
Proposed that Smith & Jones Accountants be reappointed.
*Carried unanimously.*

### 8. Special Resolution: Constitution Amendment
Proposed amendment to increase maximum Board size from 9 to 11 directors.
*Carried: 44 in favour, 2 against, 1 abstention (93.6%)*

### 9. Any Other Business
- Member asked about floodlight upgrade — Board confirmed seeking grants
- Member raised car parking concerns — under review
- Vote of thanks to volunteers

### 10. Close
Meeting closed at 9:15pm.
