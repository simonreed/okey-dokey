## Introduction

Riverside FC ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

## Data Controller

Riverside FC is the data controller for personal information processed through:
- Membership registration
- Player registration
- Event bookings
- Newsletter subscriptions
- Website usage

**Contact**: dataprotection@riversidefc.com

## Information We Collect

### Members & Players

| Data Type | Purpose | Legal Basis |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Name, address, DOB | Registration, FA affiliation | Contract, legal obligation |
| Emergency contacts | Player welfare | Legitimate interest |
| Medical information | Player safety | Vital interests |
| Photos/video | Marketing, match coverage | Consent |
| Payment details | Subscriptions, fees | Contract |

### Website Visitors

- IP address and browser information (analytics)
- Cookies (see Cookie Policy)
- Contact form submissions

## How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

- Register players with the FA and leagues
- Communicate club news and fixtures
- Process membership and match fees
- Ensure player welfare and safety
- Improve our services
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Sharing

We share data with:

| Organisation | Purpose |
|--------------|---------|
| The FA | Player registration (mandatory) |
| County FA | League registration |
| Insurance providers | Player insurance |
| Payment processors | Taking payments |

We never sell your data to third parties.

## Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| Current member/player records | Duration of membership + 6 years |
| Former member/player records | 6 years after leaving |
| Financial records | 7 years |
| Safeguarding records | 50 years or as required |
| Marketing preferences | Until withdrawn |

## Your Rights

Under data protection law, you have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data
- **Rectify** inaccurate data
- **Erase** data (in certain circumstances)
- **Restrict** processing
- **Object** to processing
- **Data portability**
- **Withdraw consent** at any time

To exercise these rights, contact: **dataprotection@riversidefc.com**

## Security

We protect your data using:

- Secure storage (encrypted where appropriate)
- Access controls
- Regular security reviews
- Staff training

## Cookies

Our website uses cookies for:
- Essential functionality
- Analytics (Google Analytics)
- Preferences

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated via email or our website.
