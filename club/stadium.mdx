## The Ground

Riverside Stadium has been home to Riverside FC since 1925. Located on River Road, just a short walk from the town centre, the ground has a current capacity of 2,500 (800 seated).

## Facilities

### Main Stand
The Main Stand houses 800 seats and provides the best view of the action. The stand includes:
- Executive boxes
- Press facilities
- Directors' lounge
- Accessible seating positions

### Terraces
Covered standing areas run along both ends of the ground, while the River Road side offers open terracing with views across the valley.

### Clubhouse
The clubhouse is open before, during, and after matches, serving:
- Hot and cold drinks
- Matchday food (burgers, hot dogs, pies)
- Club merchandise

## Getting Here

### By Car
**Address**: Riverside Stadium, River Road, Riverside, RV1 2FC

Free street parking is available on nearby residential roads (please park considerately). A small car park is available for disabled badge holders.

### By Public Transport
**Bus**: Routes 12, 15, and 23 stop on High Street (5-minute walk)
**Train**: Riverside Station is a 15-minute walk

### By Bike
Cycle racks are available by the main entrance.

## Accessibility

- Wheelchair positions available in the Main Stand
- Accessible toilet facilities
- Free carer admission
- Hearing loop in the clubhouse

Please contact the club in advance to book accessible spaces.
