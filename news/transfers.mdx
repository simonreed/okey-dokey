## Latest News

### Academy Duo Sign Professional Contracts
**3 February 2026**

We're delighted to announce that academy graduates Jack Reynolds and Mia Thompson have signed their first professional contracts with the club.

Reynolds, 17, is a versatile midfielder who has impressed in the U18s this season and has already featured for the reserves. Thompson, 16, is a striker who has scored 18 goals for the Women's U18s and made her first-team debut in November.

> "Both Jack and Mia represent everything we want from our academy — talent, dedication, and a real understanding of what it means to play for Riverside." — **David Morrison, Manager**
