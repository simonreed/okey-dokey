## Welcome to the Academy

The Riverside FC Youth Academy provides football coaching and competition for children aged 6 to 14. Our focus is on enjoyment, development, and creating a love for the game that will last a lifetime.

## Joining the Academy

New players are welcome to join throughout the season, subject to space in each age group.

### How to Register

1. Complete our online registration form
2. Attend a taster session (free of charge)
3. Pay the termly subscription
4. Collect your club kit

### Costs

- **Termly subscription**: £60 (includes training and matches)
- **Club kit**: £35 (shirt, shorts, socks)
- **Training top** (optional): £20

<Callout type="note">
We never want cost to be a barrier. Financial support is available — please speak to the Academy Director in confidence.
</Callout>
