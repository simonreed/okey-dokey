## About the Reserves

Our reserve team serves as a crucial development pathway, providing competitive football for players looking to break into the first team, those returning from injury, and talented youth players ready to step up.

The reserves compete in the Regional Reserve League, playing most fixtures on Saturday mornings.

## Squad

The reserve squad is fluid throughout the season, with players moving between the first team and reserves based on form, fitness, and development needs.

Regular members include:

- Academy graduates gaining senior experience
- First team players building match fitness
- New signings adapting to our style of play

## Fixtures

Reserve fixtures are typically played at our training ground on Saturday mornings at 11:00am. Entry is free for all supporters.
