## About the U16s

The Under-16s squad focuses on developing well-rounded players who understand both the technical and tactical demands of football. This is a crucial age for player development, as young players transition from youth football to the more physical, faster-paced game ahead.

## Philosophy

At U16 level, we prioritise:

- **Decision-making** — Thinking quickly under pressure
- **Positional play** — Understanding roles and responsibilities
- **Ball mastery** — Confidence in possession
- **Team play** — Combining effectively with teammates

## Competition

The U16s play in the County Youth League Division 1, with home matches at our training ground on Sunday mornings.

## Parent Information

Parents/guardians are expected to:

- Ensure players arrive on time for training and matches
- Support positively from the sideline
- Communicate any issues with coaching staff
- Ensure players have appropriate kit and equipment
