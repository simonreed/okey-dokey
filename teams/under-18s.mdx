## About the U18s

Our Under-18s team represents the pinnacle of our youth development pathway. Players at this level are preparing for the transition to adult football, whether that's with our reserve team, first team, or progressing to higher-level clubs.

## Development Focus

At U18 level, our coaching focuses on:

1. **Tactical understanding** — Reading the game at senior level
2. **Physical development** — Strength and conditioning for adult football
3. **Mental resilience** — Dealing with pressure and expectations
4. **Technical refinement** — Perfecting individual skills

## Matches

The U18s compete in the County Youth Premier League, with matches on Saturday mornings at 11:00am.

## Trials

We hold open trials for the U18s squad each summer (June/July). Players interested in joining should:

1. Register via our contact form
2. Attend the scheduled trial date
3. Bring appropriate kit, boots, and shinpads

<Callout type="note">
All players must have parental consent and complete our registration forms before participating in trials.
</Callout>
