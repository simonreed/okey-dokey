## About the Team

The Women's Reserves provide a vital development pathway for players looking to progress into the first team, as well as offering competitive football for those who may not be available for all first team commitments.

### Management

| Role | Name |
|------|------|
| Coach | Claire Mitchell |
| Assistant | Joanne Price |

## Who's It For?

The reserves welcome:

- **Development players** — Young players stepping up from youth football
- **Returners** — Players getting back into the game after a break
- **First team squad** — Players building fitness or match sharpness
- **New players** — Those new to women's football wanting competitive experience

## Training

Training takes place on **Wednesday evenings from 7:00pm to 8:30pm** at the training ground.

## Matches

Reserve fixtures are arranged throughout the season, typically on Sunday mornings at 10:30am. The team plays friendly matches and enters local cup competitions.

## Get Involved

Whether you've played before or are picking up football for the first time, we'd love to welcome you to training.
